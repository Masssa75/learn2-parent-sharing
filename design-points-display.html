Recommended Approach

I recommend Design 1 (Pill Badge) or Design 6 (Hover Expand) because:

  • Pill Badge: Clear, always visible, and matches the rounded aesthetic of the profile button
  • Hover Expand: Keeps the header minimal while still providing detailed info on demand
  • Both work well on mobile (hover becomes tap-to-view on mobile)
  • They don't compete with the profile button for attention
  • Easy to animate when points are earned (pulse, glow, or bounce)

For the pill badge, we could also add subtle animations:

  • Pulse glow when points are earned
  • Number count-up animation
  • Subtle shimmer effect on the border
  • Color intensity based on recent activity
